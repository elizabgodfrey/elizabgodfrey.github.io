My Work

Branding

Launched a new creative campaign for CIRCA that returned the brand to a luxury look and feel and featured supermodels Elaine Irwin and Veronica Webb. The cross-channel campaign was featured in key press and fêted with a joint CIRCA-Harper’s Bazaar launch party at Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week.

Digital Strategy

Transformed the digital presence of the Four Seasons’ CA Health and Longevity Institute by streamlining the content on the website to be more informative and user-friendly, and devising a social media content strategy that positioned CHLI as an authority on all things health and wellness.

Engagement

Launched the CIRCA Accessories Salon at Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week in partnership with the CFDA. The Salon featured a different emerging jewelry or accessories designer each day who was hand-selected by CIRCA and the CFDA. The Salon was so successful it continued for three-seasons (Feb 2011, Sept 2011, Feb 2012) and became a chic destination for designers, media, tastemakers and celebrities. It also garnered substantial national and international press coverage. Leveraged CIRCA’s social media platform during MBFW to highlight featured designers and create an interactive experience with social extensions.

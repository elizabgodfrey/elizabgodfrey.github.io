Which two websites are you most inspired by?

My final project will be to build a website for my consulting business, so I took a look at websites of similar agencies. The two I liked the most were Creative Butter and Method

How do they exhibit effective use of space, size or balance

Creative Butter does a good job of sectioning off their homepage so that all of the important information is easily accessible to the site visitor. It provides a good ’at a glance’ overview.

Method’s design is even cleaner and more straightforward, with a clear link to client case studies that showcase their work.

What makes them similar and different?

Both have a clear and basic delineation of sections, and both allow the user to scroll the homepage to obtain the pertinent information, so they are both mobile–friendly.
